Course Desscription

This course provides an in-depth exploration of social media marketing, focusing on strategy development, content creation, and audience engagement across major platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Students will learn how to design effective social media campaigns,and optimize strategies for maximum impact,preparing them to enhance brand presence and drive business growth through social media.

Learning Objectives

  • Design engaging and relevant content for various social media channels.
  • Plan, execute, and manage social media campaigns to drive brand awareness and conversions.
  • Apply concepts through practical exercises and case studies to solve real-world social media challenges.
  • Apply best practices to optimize social media strategies for improved reach and engagement.
