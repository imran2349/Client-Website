Course Desscription

Machine learning is a field of computer science that focuses on developing algorithms and techniques that allow computers to learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data. It involves creating models that can automatically learn patterns and relationships from large datasets, enabling them to improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed for every task. Machine learning is widely used in various applications such as image and speech recognition, medical diagnosis, recommendation systems, autonomous vehicles, and more, playing a crucial role in advancing technology and solving complex problems.

Learning Objectives

  • To efficiently process large volumes of data and learn from it to solve real-world problems effectively.
  • To automate tasks that are challenging to program using traditional rule-based methods, especially in complex and dynamic environments.
  • To adapt to changing conditions and new data, ensuring continuous improvement and relevance of the model.
  • To generalize from known examples and apply learned knowledge to new, unseen data, thus improving the model's ability to handle variability..
