Course Desscription

Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, theft, or damage. It encompasses measures such as encryption, firewalls, antivirus software, and intrusion detection systems to safeguard against threats like malware, phishing, and hacking. The goal of cybersecurity is to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information while preventing unauthorized access and data breaches.

Learning Objectives

  • Grasp key concepts such as confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
  • Develop strategies to mitigate risks and prevent security breaches.
  • Apply best practices for securing networks and systems.
  • Learn the steps for detecting, responding to, and recovering from cyber incidents.

Understand the cybersecurity landscape, including the role of policies, regulations, and standards,Learn methodologies for risk assessment and implement mitigation strategies to prevent and respond to security incidents,Gain practical experience with security technologies including encryption methods, firewalls, intrusion prevention systems (IPS), and antivirus solutions.
